Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 17 years or older
  • Weight: At least 110 pounds
  • Health: No recent surgeries or infections
  • Medications: Check with us if you're unsure

Donation Process

  1. Registration: Fill out a donor form.
  2. Health Screening: Brief health questionnaire and vital signs check.
  3. Donation: Blood collection takes about 10-15 minutes.
  4. Post-Donation: Rest and refreshments provided.

Types of Blood Donation

  • Whole Blood Donation
  • Plasma Donation
  • Platelet Donation
  • Double Red Cell Donation

Benefits of Donating Blood

For Recipients:

  • Saves lives and supports medical treatments.
  • Helps in emergencies and surgeries.

For Donors:

  • Improved cardiovascular health.
  • Free health screenings.
  • A sense of fulfillment and community contribution.
